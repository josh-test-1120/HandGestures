Key Findings

Motion Behavior Insights
  • • Pre-seizure indicators were observed 10–20 seconds before onset
  • • Tonic and Tremor patterns showed distinct frequency ranges (3–5Hz)
  • • Postural shifts were frequently linked with fall-like acceleration spikes
Model Performance
  • • LSTM model achieved over 98% classification accuracy
  • • Semi-supervised learning reduced manual labeling by 70%
  • • Confidence thresholding (90%) helped minimize false positives
